This week's choice - Softengine  2017.07.22

    This week I'm going to tell you how awesome this band is. Softengine, a band I discovered in Eurovision song contest 2014, although they haven't released new songs recently, I am still waiting for them. Last year I travelled to Helsinki, Finland, sadly I didn't have a chance to see their live show. I hope I can see their live show one day.
    Their first song I know is called Something better, they song brought them to the ESC, the vocal Topi was amazing on the stage, please, watch this video before you decide to like them or not.

Even though one of the member left the band earlier this year, there are only three members in the band, but I think they will come back on the stage soon, and I am really looking forward to it.
